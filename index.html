Patterns that predict non-conversion
Pattern 1: 5+ DNPs with no WA engagement
Leads with 4+ unanswered calls and no WA reply convert at under 2%. After 5 attempts with no engagement at all (not even "call later") — park. Do not continue dialling. Examples: theeonai.com (3 DNPs), ksa.jashnfoods.com (3 DNPs).
Pattern 2: "We have a CA" without specific CA complaint
If they say "we have a CA" and show zero frustration with the CA setup, the pain is not present. Only engage further if they mention: CA only comes monthly, or can't get real-time P&L, or have to chase the CA. Swadesi Way discarded correctly.
Pattern 3: Finance team confirmed in-house
Craftech 360: "they already have an accounting team." Phono Technologies: "6 member finance team." Any lead where the team has dedicated finance headcount — discard immediately with "Not our ICP." NextLeap: 4 accountants = should never have reached DS.
Pattern 4: B2B/B2G only, no D2C/marketplace
Aerospace (NextLeap, Varchas, Okulo), IT services (iOSYS, Zyple SAP), HR staffing (SHRS), Media production (SHUBHARAMBH), Consulting (Pride Circle, Frantiger). These categories have never converted and won't. Pre-filter before dialling.
Pattern 5: "Not now, maybe in 3 months"
vardhman sleepwear: "going fine, will need it when we scale." Abekus: "few months down the line." These are Parked, not discarded. Set n8n alert for: new marketplace added, job post detected. Then escalate automatically. Don't manually follow up — let signals trigger.
